RECOMMENDATION GUIDELINES:
- approve: Excellent UX (>0.75), high accessibility, low cognitive load, high adoption potential
- reject: Poor UX (<0.55), OR accessibility issues, OR high cognitive load, OR low adoption potential, such as:
